Install Subs

Add this app to your home screen in Safari — takes about 10 seconds.

1

Open the app below in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome).

2

Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow at the bottom of Safari.

3

Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add.

Open Subs App

Profile-based install doesn't work reliably on newer iPhones. Add to Home Screen is the official way and works every time.
